PayPerPoop Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2025

Thank you for using PayPerPoop ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Introduction

PayPerPoop is a fun and interactive app that tracks toilet time and calculates earnings based on user input. To enhance user experience, we collect certain data through Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob.

2. Information We Collect

When you use PayPerPoop, we may collect the following types of data:

Automatically Collected Information:

  • App usage data (e.g., session duration, features used)
  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system)
  • Advertising data (Google AdMob interactions, if personalized ads are enabled)

User-Provided Information:

  • Display name (if manually entered)
  • Friend code (for leaderboard functionality)
  • App preferences (e.g., GDPR consent, ad preferences)

This data is stored securely in Firebase and is used solely for improving user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve app functionality and performance.
  • Analyze user engagement through Firebase Analytics.
  • Display relevant ads via Google AdMob (personalized or generic).
  • Provide leaderboard rankings among friends.
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

PayPerPoop uses third-party services for analytics and ads. These services may collect user data as outlined in their respective privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google LLC)

We do not sell or share user data with any other third parties.

  • Modify your data – Update inaccurate information.
  • Delete your data – Request permanent deletion.
