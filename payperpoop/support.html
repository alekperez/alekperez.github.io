PayPerPoop Support

What the app does

PayPerPoop is a calculator that estimates what your bathroom time is worth at work based on your salary and hours. No payouts or cashouts.

Quick help

  • Restore Pro: Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases.
  • Upgrade to Pro: Statistics tab → View Poop History → Upgrade to Pro.
  • Friends & Leaderboard: Share your friend code in Settings and accept requests.
  • Purchase issues: Check your network, confirm you're signed into the correct Apple ID, and tap Restore Purchases. For refunds, contact Apple Support.

FAQ

Does the app pay me?
No. It shows an estimate of what your time is worth based on your salary and work hours.

What does Pro include?
A one-time purchase that unlocks Poop History and removes ads.

Where is my data stored?
Salary and pay settings stay on your device and aren’t shared. If you use friends/leaderboard or cloud sync, limited account data is stored to support those features.

Do you track analytics?
We may collect minimal analytics and crash data to improve the app.

How do I request data deletion?
Open Settings → Delete Account to permanently remove your cloud data. If you can't access the app, email us with subject "Delete My Data" including your username and friend code.
